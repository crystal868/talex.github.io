Order Policy


  • In Stock items require 100% payment prior to delivery or upon pick-up.

  • Pre-order and custom build items require a 50% downpayment prior to commencement.

  • Talex accepts bank deposits/online transfers and Linxs payment on pickup.

  • A copy of the payment receipt/confirmation for bank deposits/transfers must be sent to Talex via email or WhatsApp.

  • A Site Visit is required for custom pieces. Site visits carry a fee which will be confirmed prior to scheduling and must be paid prior to the visit.

  • Should you wish to forgo the site visit and provide your own measurements, a Waiver of Site Visit Form must be completed and signed.

  • Talex will confirm whether a fee is required for an estimate. Any estimate fee must be paid at the time of booking the site visit/submission of the waiver.

Pick Up & Delivery Policy


  • Pick-up is at Talex’s Workshop/Warehouse.

  • Pickup must be scheduled between Talex’s operating hours (Monday to Saturday).

  • Confirmation of Pickup must be made at least 24hrs in advance of collection.


  • Talex retains the services of external independent delivery service providers.

  • Upon submission of the delivery location a rough quote for delivery will be provided.

  • You will be provided with the delivery agent’s contact number and directed to confirm delivery time, location etc.

  • Payment of the delivery fee is due in cash and payable to the delivery driver.
