How to Order

Click to View

In-Stock Items

1. Take note of product number, color and other preferences
2. Complete and submit order form
3. Make payment and submit payment confirmation (if paying via bank deposit/online transfer) to Talex.
4. You will be contacted within one (1) business day with further instructions for pick-up/delivery.

Pre-Order Items

1. Take note of product number, color and other preferences.
2. Complete and submit order form
3. Make downpayment (50%) and submit payment confirmation to Talex.
4. You will receive a receipt for the downpayment within 24hr of credit to Talex’s account.
5. You will be contacted when the order is ready for pickup/delivery.
6. Balance of payment (50%) is required prior to delivery or upon delivery or pick-up.

Custom Orders

1. Complete the information request form (Contact Us page) with detailed information on the requested item – color, size, style etc. You may send an inspiration picture.
2. Schedule a Site Visit or complete the Waiver of Site Visit Form.
3. Make payment for Site Visit/Estimate Fee and submit payment confirmation to Talex.
4. Site Visit will be conducted as per mutual arrangement with client.
5. Talex will provide a quote for the item within 48-72hrs of the Site Visit.
6. Make 50% downpayment on project and submit payment confirmation to Talex.
7. You will receive a receipt for the downpayment within 24hr of credit to Talex’s account.
8. You will be contacted when the order is ready for pickup/delivery.
9. Balance of payment (50%) is required prior to delivery or upon delivery or pick-up.
