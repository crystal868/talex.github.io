Meet the Founders

A Union of Talent & Passion

Talex Interior Solutions, founded by husband-and-wife team Josiah and Crystal Alexander, embodies their commitment to craftsmanship and artistic excellence in custom and curated interior solutions. Originating from Josiah's 25 years of experience in joinery and a personal need to optimize space in their family home, the business emerged from the couple's dedication to creating tailored, innovative furniture and accessories that address common challenges faced by households. The name "Talex" reflects a fusion of their surnames, symbolizing the union of their talents and passions while establishing a legacy of quality that resonates with clients seeking innovative interior solutions.

Josiah Alexander

Founder & Head of Operations

Josiah Alexander, Co-Founder and Operations Manager of Talex Interior Solutions, boasts nearly thirty years of experience in crafting custom designs for residential and commercial spaces. His unwavering commitment to high-quality, tailored solutions reflects his dedication to innovation and adaptability in a dynamic industry, ensuring that his work not only meets but exceeds client expectations. Driven by a passion for design and craftsmanship, Josiah focuses on creating functional and visually appealing pieces that enhance user experience and enrich everyday life..

Crystal Alexander

Co-Founder & Finance & Administration Manager

Crystal Alexander, as Co-founder and Finance and Administrative Manager at Talex, leverages over fifteen years of retail customer service experience to ensure excellence in all operations. Her dedication to outstanding customer service, effective communication, and efficient administration positions Talex as a potential industry leader focused on exceptional client experiences and long-term relationships.
